CASE STUDY

WHEN TECHNOLOGY WASN'T
THE PROBLEM

A multi-entity enterprise had fully implemented Oracle ERP across Finance and Operations. The system was live. Users were active. Reports were available.

Yet performance had stalled. Approval cycles were slow. Manual reconciliations continued outside the system. Spreadsheets supplemented executive reporting.

Leadership questioned the return on their Oracle investment. The technology was functioning. Structure was not.

Tekafriq conducted a structured workflow and control review, identifying approval bottlenecks, unclear decision rights, and redundant manual processes that had been automated rather than redesigned.

We recalibrated approval hierarchies, eliminated parallel tracking systems, re-optimized configurations, and embedded accountability into process ownership.

TECHNOLOGY WAS NEVER THE CONSTRAINT. STRUCTURE WAS.

  • ✓ ELIMINATION OF OFF-SYSTEM RECONCILIATIONS
  • ✓ IMPROVED REPORTING ACCURACY AND AUDIT READINESS
